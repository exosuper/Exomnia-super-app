The Problem

Modern collaboration is fragmented.

Students and professionals waste hours switching between apps that don't talk to each other. EXOMNIA fixes this.

📁

File Choice

Documents scattered across email, Slack, Dropbox, Google Drive — no central hub. Finding the right file wastes precious time.

🔀

Disconnected Tools

Meetings in Zoom, tasks in Asana, boards in Miro — constant app-switching kills focus and productivity.

🌐

Weak Networks

Students and professionals can't easily build meaningful cross-industry connections on a single platform.

⏰

No Accountability

Task deadlines and submissions lack seamless, real-time tracking and feedback loops — work falls through the cracks.
