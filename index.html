⚡ The Problem

App Overload Is Killing Productivity

Students and corporate employees lack a single unified space for video calls with whiteboard-style idea presentation, real-time collaboration, and professional social networking. Switching between Zoom, Miro, LinkedIn, and Instagram every day drains focus and energy.
